If \(\mu _Q.\mathrm{colLen}(0) \geq \mu _P.\mathrm{colLen}(0)\) (primitive case), then

\[ |\mathrm{PBP}_C(\mu _P, \mu _Q)| = |\mathrm{PBP}_D(\mu _P, \mathrm{shiftLeft}(\mu _Q))|. \]
Proof ▶

Under \(\mu _Q.\mathrm{colLen}(0) \geq \mu _P.\mathrm{colLen}(0)\), every D-type on \((\mu _P, \mathrm{shiftLeft}(\mu _Q))\) is in the image of the C\(\to \)D descent; injectivity from Lemma 38.

  • card_C_eq_DD_plus_RC_balanced
  • prop_10_8_C_balanced

If \(\mu _P.\mathrm{colLen}(0) = \mu _Q.\mathrm{colLen}(0) + 1\) (balanced case), the C\(\to \)D descent image is \(\{ \sigma \in \mathrm{PBP}_D \mid \mathrm{tailClass}(\sigma ) \in \{ \mathrm{DD}, \mathrm{RC}\} \} \):

\[ |\mathrm{PBP}_C(\mu _P, \mu _Q)| = \# \{ \mathrm{DD}\} + \# \{ \mathrm{RC}\} . \]
Proof ▶

The image of the C\(\to \)D descent in the balanced case consists exactly of the D-type PBPs with tail class \(\mathrm{DD}\) or \(\mathrm{RC}\): a \(\mathrm{DD}\) tail corresponds to lifted \(c\) in \(\mathcal{P}\) col 0, \(\mathrm{RC}\) to lifted \(r\). Summing cardinalities gives the claim.

If \(\mu _P.\mathrm{colLen}(0) {\gt} \mu _Q.\mathrm{colLen}(0)\) (primitive):

\[ |\mathrm{PBP}_M(\mu _P, \mu _Q)| = |\mathrm{PBP}_{B^+}(\mathrm{shiftLeft}(\mu _P), \mu _Q)| + |\mathrm{PBP}_{B^-}(\mathrm{shiftLeft}(\mu _P), \mu _Q)|. \]
Proof ▶

Descending M\(\to \)B at a primitive step splits into \(B^+\) and \(B^-\) branches according to the bottom-\(\mathcal{Q}\) paint. Injectivity of each branch combined with the disjoint branches gives the sum.

\[ |\mathrm{PBP}_M(\mu _P, \mu _Q)| = |\mathrm{PBP}_{B^+}(\cdots )| + |\{ \sigma \in \mathrm{PBP}_{B^-}(\cdots ) : \mathrm{layerOrd}(\sigma .\mathcal{Q}(c_2 - 1, 0)) {\gt} 1\} |. \]
