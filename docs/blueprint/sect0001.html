1 Introduction

This blueprint documents the Lean 4 formalization of the combinatorial aspects of associated cycles of special unipotent representations, following two papers by Barbasch–Ma–Sun–Zhu:

  • [BMSZb] — counting reduction paper (Section 10): PBP counting recursion formulas for classical groups.

  • [BMSZ] — construction and unitarity paper (Section 11): combinatorial associated cycles \(\mathcal{L}_\tau \).

The formalization covers \(\star \in \{ B, D, C, M = \tilde{C}\} \); real-form variants \(C^*\), \(D^*\) are deliberately skipped.
