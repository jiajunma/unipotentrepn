4.1 Single naive descent paints

There are four single descent directions:

  • D \(\to \) C: cut col 0 of \(\mathcal{Q}\) and shift \(\mathcal{P}\) left by one column (refill top with \(\bullet \)).

  • C \(\to \) D: cut col 0 of \(\mathcal{P}\) (refill top/middle with \(\bullet , s\)).

  • B \(\to \) M: cut col 0 of \(\mathcal{Q}\) (refill \(\mathcal{P}\), translate).

  • M \(\to \) B: cut col 0 of \(\mathcal{P}\).

Let \(c_L(j) := \mathrm{dotScolLen}(\tau .\mathcal{P}, j + 1)\) and \(c_R(j) := \tau .\mathcal{Q}.\mathrm{colLen}(j)\).

\begin{align*} (\nabla _{DC}\tau ).\mathcal{P}(i, j) & = \begin{cases} \bullet & i {\lt} c_L(j) \\ \tau .\mathcal{P}(i, j + 1) & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}\\ (\nabla _{DC}\tau ).\mathcal{Q}(i, j) & = \begin{cases} \bullet & i {\lt} c_L(j) \\ s & c_L(j) \leq i {\lt} c_R(j) \\ \bullet & i \geq c_R(j) \end{cases}\end{align*}
